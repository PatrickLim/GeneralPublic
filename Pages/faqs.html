The overall environment in which many Americans now live, work, learn, and play has contributed to the obesity epidemic, because food choices are often limited – especially at work and school.                    Some sugars are found naturally in foods, such as lactose in milk and fructose in fruit, whereas others are added to foods, such as table sugar and high fructose corn syrup.
