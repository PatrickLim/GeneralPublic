Café Pavilion


Café Pavilion is our state of the art self checkout automated Café setup where we offer the public our Coffee Connection & Tea Leaves as well as a variety of bakery selections, small sandwhiches, and soft beverages.

 

Café Pavilion is ideal for every location, anywhere from the workplace, school, office building, airports, hospitals, hotels, and thousands of other locations.

 

Café Pavilion Level 1 features 2 sectional units. (from right to left):

The first section includes a commercial grade Keurig brewing machine alongside a cup dispenser, sweeteners, half and half, and other condiment items. Above the Keurig brewing machine, there is a compartment for a variety of single "K-Cup" coffees and teas. The second section consists of a compartment for bakery goods located above the user interface panel and the assorted products and personal items.

 

Café Pavilion Level 2 features 3 Sectional units. (from right to left):

The first section includes a commercial grade Keurig brewing machine alongside a cup dispenser, sweeteners, half and half, and other condiment items. The second section has a refrigerator unit for soft drinks, small sandwiches dairy products, and pre-cut vegetables and salads. Above the refrigerator is a compartment for bakery goods. The third section consists of an additional compartment for bakery goods located above the user interface panel and the assorted products and personal items.

 

Café Pavilion Level 3 features 4 sectional units (from right to left):

The first section includes a commercial grade Keurig brewing machine alongside a cup dispenser, sweeteners, half and half, and other condiment items. The second section consists of a full sized refrigerator for a variety of soft drinks, small sandwiches dairy products, and pre-cut vegetables and salads. The third section offers a full sized compartment for bakery goods, snacks, and other dry goods. The fourth section consists the user interface panel and the assorted products and personal items.
